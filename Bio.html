Hi there...

I’m Steffan, in the next couple of lines you’ll probably know more about me than I care to remember.

Hopefully as you can tell I like making people laugh and I myself, love having a good time, which in turn is the basis that drives me to develop small games in my past time. For the few brief moments, in which I can get my brain to focus on something other than food, I tend to find myself conceptualizing witty little games that are easy to create and fun enough for my friends to play and still remain as friends.

But during the rest of my day you can usually find me outside playing basketball or some other sport or I’d probably be at the gym. Although ever since the release of “Pokémon Go” I’ve been more inclined to walk around my town catching imaginary creatures, on my smartphone than any other form of activity, as sad as that may sound.

But who am I really?

I am a growing programmer who loves interacting with languages such as JAVA and HTML to create what my mind cooks up. After the completion of my degree from the University of Northampton I plan to become a programmer or a coder professionally.

You can find out more about me through my social media such as Twitter, GitHub and the rest of the links below.
